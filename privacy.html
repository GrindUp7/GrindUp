We collect several types of information from and about users of our Site, including:

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our Site, including:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information collected about you for various purposes:

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary:

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to request correction or deletion
  • Right to restrict processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@grindup.com.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Stripe (payment processing)

7. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.
